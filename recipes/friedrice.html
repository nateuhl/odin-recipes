Japanese Fried Rice

Back to Recipes


It should be no surprise that rice is a staple dish in Asian culture. Today we're
going to try out this homemade japanese fried rice. It's food for the soul.

Let's start with the ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 cups of cooked jasmine rice
  • 2 tbsp Kewpie Japanese Mayonnaise
  • 2 slices short cut bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup green shallots
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Pepper to taste

Time to Cook

  1. In a small bowl, mix the mayonnaise well with the cooked rice.

  2. Chop the green shallots and bacon into small pieces.

  3. Heat a little olive oil in a frying pan over high heat then add the rice and fry for a few minutes.

  4. Put the rice to one side of the pan and add the eggs and scramble them.

  5. Once the egg is half cooked, stir it into the rice.

  6. Add the bacon and cook for about 5 minutes.

  7. Add the shallots and salt and stir for a bit.

  8. Remove from the heat and sprinkle a bit of pepper to taste.

  9. Grab your chopsticks and enjoy!
